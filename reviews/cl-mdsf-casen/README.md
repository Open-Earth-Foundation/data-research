# Encuesta CASEN (MDSF)

CASEN is Chile’s national socioeconomic household survey, published by the
Ministerio de Desarrollo Social y Familia via Observatorio Social.

## Why we use it
Primary source for:
- median household income (proxy for purchasing power / vulnerability)
- poverty (income-based and multidimensional) and inequality indicators
- unemployment / labour market indicators
- commuting mode shares (incl. public transport)

## Current approved release
**2022**

## Main sources
- CASEN 2022 landing page (results + docs): https://observatorio.ministeriodesarrollosocial.gob.cl/encuesta-casen-2022
- Pobreza comunal 2022: https://observatorio.ministeriodesarrollosocial.gob.cl/pobreza-comunal-2022

## Notes
CASEN is periodic (every 2–3 years) and product availability differs by topic
(e.g., income, poverty, labour). Some outputs are updated or re-issued after
initial publication.
